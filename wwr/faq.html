Frequently Asked Questions

We are often asked what the best rafts are. The answer is it really depends. The number of users is an imortant factor. Where you are going. it really doesn't matter. rent the right sized one and you will have a great time.

Where is the best place to go? do something slow to start. taking time and getting used to it is important and will lead you to enjoying and being able to take on the faster ones.

What do I need to do to prepare? There are a lot of things needed to be done in preperation. choosing a raft, picking a place to go, having enough food and other necissary recources. take your time, make a list. be intentional and your trip will be a success.
